| 1 | Minto 金字塔原理：answer-first；SCQA（Situation/Complication/Question/Answer）為開場結構；MECE（互斥窮盡）分組；垂直＋水平邏輯；想法四種排序（演繹/時序/結構/比較）；忌無結構「Background」段 | ✅ 確認 | [modelthinkers](https://modelthinkers.com/mental-model/minto-pyramid-scqa)、[strategyu](https://strategyu.co/pyramid-principle-partone/)、[antoinebuteau](https://www.antoinebuteau.com/lessons-from-barbara-minto/) |
